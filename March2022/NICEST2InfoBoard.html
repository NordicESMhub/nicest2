A MESSAGE FROM THE NICEST 2 PROJECT MANAGER

NICEST2 InfoBoard 

You will find below the 5 most important news and outcomes of the NICEST 2 project.

In-person hackathon on EDAM-geo 3-5 May 2022, Oslo (Norway): we are organising a 3 days hackathon to extend the EDAM ontology for Geosciences. This in-person event will take place in Oslo (Norway). More information and registration here.

NeIC conference 2022 will take place in Oslo (Norway) from 30th May 2022 to 1st June 2022. Come and hear about the Climate Community research and data management: session is on 31st May 2022. You can register to the conference here: https://www.deltager.no/event/neic2022 

Training on Open Science with EOSC, 2-3 May 2022, just after the NeIC 2022 conference, the Climate Community is organising a workshop to show you how to implement Open Science practices using EOSC services. It is a free and open to everyone event. Registration is coming soon.

GPU and Climate Science. Do you have any resources, training, webinars on GPUs for Climate Science? Or are you using GPUs for Climate Science? We are aiming at establishing a GPU working group for Climate Science in the Nordic. Reach out jeani@uio.no if you are interested.

ESMValTool diagnostics & NICEST 2: we have reached out to researchers who were interested in getting support from NICEST 2 to integrate their diagnostics to ESMVALTool. Stay tuned!

Contact: annefou@geo.uio.no, Project Manager of the NICEST2 project.
