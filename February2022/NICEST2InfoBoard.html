A MESSAGE FROM THE NICEST 2 PROJECT MANAGER

NICEST2 InfoBoard 

You will find below the most important news and outcomes of the NICEST 2 project.

ESMValTool survey: 16 answers only from researchers. 12 do not use ESMValTool. 2 have already developed ESMValTool recipes and 6 researchers shared their codes.

Bi-weekly ESGF Operation meetings are back!

Upcoming online workshop with ESGF Operation Team and Nordic researchers to exchange on Climate data publishing. The date is not set. Tell us if you want to attend!

FAIR for Climate data and software: we are planning the next steps after a very successful M4M (metadata for Machine) workshop with EOSC-Nordic. Everyone is welcome to attend and contribute.

We would like to invite HPC national providers and the climate research community to discuss on our respective roles towards EuroHPC. This may even be submitted as a neIC pre-study: reach out if interested!

Contact: annefou@geo.uio.no, Project Manager of the NICEST2 project.
